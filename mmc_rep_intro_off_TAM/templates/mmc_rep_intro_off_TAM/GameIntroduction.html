Games, Actions and Points

  • In each period of a round, you will participate in two Games simultaneously,
    Red Game
    with Other Red and
    Blue Game
    with Other Blue.
  • For each game, you can choose between two Actions: Action
    A
    and Action
    B
    in
    Red Game
    ; Action
    W
    and Action
    Y
    in
    Blue Game
    .
  • For each Game, your points will be decided by your action and the action of the other participant.
  • You, and Other need to choose and confirm your actions in each period in a timely manner. If you do not confirm your actions in a timely manner, you will be given a warning. After the warning you will have another 30 seconds. If you do not choose and confirm your actions in the 30 seconds after the warning, an action will be chosen randomly for each game for that round.
  • The tables below show the points corresponding to your actions and the other participant's actions in each game and they will remain unchanged across all the matches in the experiment.
