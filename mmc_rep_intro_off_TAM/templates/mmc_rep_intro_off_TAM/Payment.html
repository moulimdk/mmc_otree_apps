  • After the end of each round you can see the history of the round.

  • Total Points in Experiment = Sum of Total Points in every round.
  • At the end of the experiment, you can see the total points that you earned in the experiment and its dollar equivalent.
  • Total Payment from Session = Participation payment + Quiz payment + Dollar equivalent of total points from experiment
