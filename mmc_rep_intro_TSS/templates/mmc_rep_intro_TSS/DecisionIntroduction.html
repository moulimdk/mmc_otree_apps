Taking Actions

  • You can see the Point tables and the History tables when you take actions in each period of each round.
  • On the page where you take actions, there will be buttons for the actions for each game as you can see in the figure below.
  • When you choose an action, it will appear in a statement below the buttons.
  • When you have chosen an action in each game, a button "Confirm Actions" will appear. You can change your actions as many times as you want before pressing "Confirm Actions" button.
  • Pressing the "Confirm Actions" button will confirm your actions for that period.
