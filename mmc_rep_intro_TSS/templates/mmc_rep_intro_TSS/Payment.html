Payment

  • After the end of each round you can see the history of the round.
  • Total Points in a Round = Sum of Points in Red Game over all periods in the round + Sum of Points in Blue Game over all periods in the round
  • Total Points in Experiment = Sum of Total Points in every round.
  • At the end of the experiment, you can see the total points that you earned in the experiment and its dollar equivalent.
  • Total Payment from Session = Participation payment + Quiz payment + Dollar equivalent of total points from experiment
  • If you do not choose and confirm your actions in a period in a timely manner you will be "disconnected" and can not proceed with the rest of the session.
  • If you are "disconnected" in the experiment you will be paid the participation payment and the amount you earned in the incentivized quiz.
  • What happens when either Other Red, Other Blue or both Other Red and Other Blue drop out in a round?
    • It is possible that any or both of the participants you are paired with may not take decisions in a timely manner in a period of the round (drop out).
    • If Other Red drops out, you will be allotted {{ expPoints_Red }} points for Red Game for each of the remaining periods of that round.
    • If Other Blue drops out, you will be allotted {{ expPoints_Blue }} points for Blue Game respectively for each of the remaining periods of that round.


You can press the ''Quiz'' button below to proceed to the Quiz.
